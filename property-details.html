Services

Airport & City Transfers, Taxi Services

At LA FORTUNA Hotel Taksim, we offer our guests comfortable and reliable airport transfers both to and from the hotel, as well as city transfers and 24/7 taxi services. Our professional drivers ensure a safe and pleasant journey, whether you are arriving in Istanbul or exploring the city.

City Tours: Discover the best of Istanbul with our exclusive guided tours. We organize special programs to the city's most iconic landmarks, including Galata Tower, the Bosphorus, and Hagia Sophia Mosque. Let us help you experience the magic of Istanbul!

You can request a transfer or city tour at the reception or by contacting us before your arrival. Our team will be happy to arrange everything for you.

Our transfer service includes a private vehicle, professional driver, and luggage assistance. We offer both airport-hotel and hotel-airport transfers, as well as city transfers and taxi services.

Our city tours are designed for you to discover Istanbul’s most beautiful sights with comfort and expert guidance. Enjoy a unique experience with our carefully prepared programs!
